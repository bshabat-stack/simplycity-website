What a local SEO agency actually does for your business

Local SEO is the set of signals Google uses to decide which businesses appear in the Map Pack — the three listings shown at the top of search results when someone searches ‘plumber near me’ or ‘best coffee shop nearby.’ Those three spots capture the majority of clicks before anyone scrolls further.

Ranking there requires consistent work across four areas: your Google Business Profile, your local citations, your reviews, and your website content. According to Google's own documentation, relevance, distance, and prominence are the three core ranking factors in local search — and all three are directly influenced by the work we do. Most small business owners don't have time to manage any of them. We do it for you.

“A fully optimized Google Business Profile can generate 7x more clicks than an incomplete one, according to a 2023 BrightLocal study of 10,000 local business listings.”

Our local SEO services

Google Business Profile Optimization
Your Google Business Profile is the single most important ranking factor in local search. SimplyCity audits your current profile, corrects every gap, writes keyword-optimized descriptions, selects the right business categories, and maintains a posting schedule that keeps your profile active. A complete, well-managed profile consistently outranks neglected ones — regardless of how long a competitor has been listed.
Local Citation Building and Cleanup
A citation is any mention of your business name, address, and phone number online. Google cross-references citations across hundreds of directories to verify your business and confirm its location. Inconsistent citations — a wrong phone number on Yelp, an old address on a directory — suppress your rankings. We build new citations on high-authority directories and correct the ones that are wrong.
Review Generation and Management
Review count and recency are two of the top three local ranking factors Google uses. SimplyCity builds a review generation system that asks satisfied customers for feedback at the right moment, routes negative feedback privately before it reaches public review platforms, and gives you a dashboard to monitor and respond across Google, Yelp, and Facebook.
Local Rank Tracking and Reporting
Standard rank trackers show one position for a keyword. Local search doesn't work that way — your Google Maps ranking changes by street, by neighborhood, and by search context. Our rank tracker renders a grid-level view of where you rank across your entire service area, updated weekly, so you see exactly where you're winning customers and where you're losing them.

Why local SEO matters more than it did five years ago

Google processes over 8.5 billion searches per day, and nearly half contain local intent, according to Google's own search data. Since Google expanded the Map Pack to dominate above-the-fold results on mobile, the three businesses appearing there capture an estimated 44% of all local search clicks, per a 2023 BrightLocal consumer survey.

The businesses in those three spots are not the best in their category. They are the ones with the most complete and consistent local presence. That is a solvable problem with the right agency.
